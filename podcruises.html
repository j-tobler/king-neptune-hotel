Smooth, relaxing, memorable

Explore the ocean in comfort and luxury with our very own pod cruises. These two-seated mini-submarines are perfect for ocean safaris, meditative journeys, romantic dates with a loved one or exploring the islands. With a dual-turbine motor and self-navigating technology, you can sit back and relax whilst passing by the surrounding marine life.

A real adventure

The journey you’ll take on your pod cruise will explore the entire perimeter of the ocean. As a result, you’ll be treated to a range of marine life, including jellyfish, grey nurse sharks, cuttlefish, turtles, and over 550 reef fish, 90 hard coral and 600 mollusc species. With one full hour to complete your journey, you’ll have plenty of opportunity to observe this vast array of marine life. Book a pod cruise with your accomodation package when you book online, or call us to book in advance.
