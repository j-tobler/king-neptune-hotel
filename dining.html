Unparalleled dining

Our one-of-a-kind underwater restaurant boasts 180 degree views of the open ocean, providing a backdrop to your dining occasion like no other restaurant on Earth. Our specially designed, bulletproof, military-grade glass panels are designed to withstand pressures of up to 20 times that of the surrounding ocean, so you can feel safe and secure while enjoying your meal. With luxurious seating and decor, friendly and efficient table service and a huge variety of marine life to capture your attention beyond the glass, dining with us will make for the experience of a lifetime.

Cuisine, right at the source

We take pride in serving only the highest quality meals, served by our award-winning chefs. Our restaurant provides a variety of meals, from vegetarian, to seafood, to beef and chicken based dishes. Sourced just a few kilometres East from the restaurant itself, we offer some of the freshest seafood offered by any restaurant, with the vast majority of our seafood caught within 24 hours from serving. Featured in Food Magazine, critics have praised our food for its “jaw dropping flavours” and “melt-in-your-mouth textures”, as well as our “excellent customer service and overall dining experience”. You won’t forget these flavours!
